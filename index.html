FAQ

What should we wear?

Closed-toe shoes and comfortable outdoor clothing you don’t mind getting paint on. We recommend long sleeves/pants.

Is it safe?

Yes. You’ll get a safety briefing, and our referees supervise games. Masks must stay on in the field at all times.

Do you host private events?

Absolutely — birthdays, bachelor/bachelorette, and team-building. Use the form and we’ll plan the best format.
